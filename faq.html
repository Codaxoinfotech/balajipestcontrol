General Questions

What is Balaji Pest Control?

Balaji Pest Control is a professional pest management company serving Raipur and surrounding areas in Chhattisgarh. We provide comprehensive pest control solutions for homes, offices, and commercial establishments using safe, effective, and eco-friendly methods.

Which areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Raipur city and surrounding areas including Prem Nagar, Mowa, Shankar Nagar, Pandri, Telibandha, Devendra Nagar, Kota, Tatibandh, and other locations within a 50km radius of Raipur city center.

What types of pests do you control?

We control all major pests including:

  • Termites (Dimak)
  • Cockroaches
  • Rats and Mice (Chuha)
  • Ants (Chiti)
  • Mosquitoes (Machhar)
  • Lizards (Chhipkali)
  • Bees and Wasps (Madhumakhi)
  • Spiders and other crawling insects

Do you provide services for both residential and commercial properties?

Yes, we provide pest control services for both residential homes and commercial establishments including offices, restaurants, hotels, schools, hospitals, warehouses, and industrial facilities.

Safety & Health

Are your pest control treatments safe for children and pets?

Yes, absolutely! We use only WHO-approved, eco-friendly chemicals that are safe for children and pets. Our treatments are designed to eliminate pests while ensuring the safety of your family. We also provide specific safety instructions and precautions for each treatment.

What chemicals do you use?

We use only certified, government-approved pesticides that meet international safety standards. All our chemicals are:

  • WHO approved
  • Eco-friendly and biodegradable
  • Low toxicity to humans and pets
  • Highly effective against target pests
  • Odorless or with minimal odor

Do I need to vacate my home during treatment?

For most treatments, you don't need to vacate your home. However, we may recommend staying away from treated areas for 2-4 hours depending on the type of treatment. For fumigation or intensive treatments, temporary evacuation might be necessary, which we'll inform you about in advance.

Can I stay in the house during pest control treatment?

Yes, for most of our treatments, you can stay in the house. We use targeted application methods that allow you to remain in untreated areas. Our technicians will guide you on which areas to avoid during and immediately after treatment.

Service Details

How long does a pest control treatment take?

Treatment duration varies by service type:

  • General pest control: 1-2 hours
  • Termite treatment: 2-4 hours
  • Cockroach control: 1-2 hours
  • Rat control: 1-3 hours
  • Mosquito fogging: 30-60 minutes
  • Sanitization: 1-2 hours

How effective are your treatments?

Our treatments are highly effective with a success rate of over 95%. We use proven methods and quality chemicals to ensure complete pest elimination. Most customers see significant results within 24-48 hours of treatment.

Do you provide follow-up services?

Yes, follow-up services are included in all our packages. We typically schedule follow-up visits after 15-30 days to ensure the treatment's effectiveness and provide additional treatment if needed at no extra cost during the guarantee period.

What is included in your pest control service?

Our comprehensive service includes:

  • Detailed inspection of the property
  • Identification of pest species and infestation level
  • Customized treatment plan
  • Professional application of treatments
  • Follow-up visits as per guarantee terms
  • Prevention advice and recommendations
  • Customer support and guidance

Emergency Services

What constitutes a pest control emergency?

Pest control emergencies include:

  • Large bee or wasp swarms near living areas
  • Severe rodent infestations affecting health/safety
  • Venomous spider or snake sightings
  • Massive termite swarms
  • Pest-related health emergencies
  • Urgent sanitization needs

Do you provide 24/7 emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency pest control services. Call our emergency number 88398 93211 anytime for urgent situations. Emergency services may have additional charges depending on the time and nature of the service.

How quickly do you respond to emergencies?

For genuine emergencies, we aim to respond within 30-60 minutes during regular hours and within 1-2 hours during off-hours, depending on your location and the nature of the emergency.
